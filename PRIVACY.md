# Playa Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 20, 2026

Playa is an iOS app for discovering events, saving tickets, chatting, and managing a local profile.

## Data we may collect

- Account data such as name, username, email, avatar, language, and city when the user signs in.
- User content such as posts, comments, event chats, direct chat messages, saved events, and reports.
- App settings such as language, notification preferences, saved events, local tickets, and bonus star balance.
- Basic diagnostic information needed to keep authentication, database access, and account deletion working.

## How we use data

We use this data to run the app, show event recommendations, keep profile/settings state, support chats and reports, and handle account deletion.

## Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data may be processed by our infrastructure providers, including Supabase and Apple services used for authentication and TestFlight/App Store distribution.

## User controls

Users can sign out, update local profile/settings, report content, and request account deletion from the app settings.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact support@playahub.app.
