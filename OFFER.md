# Playa Public Offer

Effective date: June 20, 2026

This document describes the commercial status of Playa build 16.

Version 1.0 does not sell digital goods, subscriptions, event tickets, or paid stars inside the iOS app. Bonus stars in this build are free review/TestFlight credits used to test ticket reservation flows.

Real-money digital goods will only be enabled after StoreKit products are configured in App Store Connect and submitted for Apple review.

For questions, contact support@playahub.app.
