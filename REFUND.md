# Playa Refund Policy

Effective date: June 20, 2026

Playa version 1.0 does not process real-money in-app purchases, paid subscriptions, or paid ticket sales.

Bonus stars in build 14 are free review/TestFlight credits and have no cash value.

If future StoreKit purchases are enabled, refunds will be handled through Apple's standard App Store refund process.

For questions, contact support@playahub.app.
